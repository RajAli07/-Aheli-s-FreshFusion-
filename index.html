About Us

We've been making healthy food last for 10 years

At Aheli, we believe that food is more than just sustenance – it's a way to nourish body, mind, and soul. Our name, Aheli, means "peaceful" in Sanskrit, reflecting our commitment to serving wholesome, delicious meals that promote well-being.

Our Story

Aheli was born from a passion for traditional Indian cuisine and a desire to make healthy eating accessible to all. Our founders,Raj Ali, grew up surrounded by the vibrant flavors and aromas of Indian cooking. They recognized the need for a modern, health-conscious approach to traditional recipes.
